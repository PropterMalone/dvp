# Session Kickoff

1. Check your memory files in `~/.claude/` for context from previous sessions
2. Briefly state what you remember about ongoing work or interests
3. Ask: "What are we working on today?"

Keep the orientation short — a few sentences max. Don't recite everything you remember, just the relevant bits.
